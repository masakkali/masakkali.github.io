How old do I have to be to become a volunteer?

We belive that age is just a number, so you can be of any age, that does not matter.

Please fill in the contact form, found at the bottom of the webiste with your details, and we will contact you.

Minimum 3 months i.e 12 weeks.

Yes, Ofcourse.
