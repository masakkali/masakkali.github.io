Conditions of Participation

These are some common FAQ's about Masakkali and how to join us.

How old can I be?

We belive that age is just a number, so you can be of any age, that does not matter.

Can we donate clothes?

Yes Ofcourse, Any form cloth donation is acceptable. Only thing that you should remember that, the item should be wearable and not in very poor condition.

What is duration for Voluntering.?

Minimum 3 months i.e 12 weeks.

Working Hours ?

2-3 Hours on weekends. Sometimes more, depending upon the situation.

Will I get a certificate on completion >

Yes, Ofcourse.

Where is your NGO located ?

Mayapuri, Delhi-15. Raen Basera. Nearest metro station Mayapuri, pink line.

Do I need any pre requisites?

No, Only dedication to work hard and an open heart.

How to apply ?

No, apart from your traveling expenses nothing will be spend from your side.
